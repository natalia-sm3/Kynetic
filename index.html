Kynetik

A concept brand focused on safer, smarter, and more engaging dog toys through design, engineering, and canine-centered innovation.

Who we are

Kynetik is a student-developed concept created to rethink dog toys in a more complete way. Instead of seeing a toy only as entertainment, we see it as a product that can support dental health, safety, durability, and cognitive stimulation at the same time.

Our project was developed as part of an engineering and design challenge, combining market research, mathematical modeling, and product proposal development.

Our mission

To design dog toys that are not only fun, but also safer for chewing, more durable in daily use, and more stimulating for dogs over time.

Our vision

To become a brand that connects engineering, responsible materials, and animal wellbeing in products that truly improve the experience of both dogs and their owners.

What makes Kynetik different

  • Focus on safer chewing and lower dental risk
  • Design backed by research and user survey analysis
  • Mathematical modeling in MATLAB to optimize geometry
  • Interest in sustainable and dog-safe materials
  • Product ideas that combine play with mental stimulation

About our product concept

Our current concept is a chew toy with a balanced geometry designed to distribute force better during play. It was developed after analyzing dog owner surveys, studying toy safety problems, and testing design ideas through interpolation and 3D modeling.

The goal is simple: create a toy that is more thoughtful than what is usually found in the market.
